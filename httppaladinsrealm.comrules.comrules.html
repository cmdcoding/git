RULES

Rules

⍜Current PPL- players are not allowed to compete in 5v5 Siege Tournaments
If you are in PPL contact me and I'll see what I can do

Banned areas:
Rooftops/abusing any areas which has a gravity mechanic by resetting with androxus hover/drogoz hover etc
Windows of Ascension Peak Corner of point in Ascension Peak where you can walk in the statue
Trees of Frog Isle
Stone Keep top of a pillar (teleporting there with Kinessa)

Reporting
Match Wins
After each set, the captains of the winning team are to report their set score
Match reports must be reported in the format
Winning Team vs Losing Team | Map Score | Match ID and a screenshot of the post-game screen
Registration & Check-in Rules

Captains are in charge of the team, they register the team, report wins and pauses, etc...
All captains are required to join the Paladins Realm Discord: https://discord.gg/rR6Pbm3
Captains must go to #role-assignment and grab the role that best suits the tournament they are looking to compete in
In order to compete the captain needs to register for the tournament on the website using the Google Forms
During check-ins, the captains must go to the #check-in channel and state “Team Name-checking in”
For any questions not covered send me a message

Siege Rules

Format: Single Elimination best of 1, and finals best of 3
Game setup:
Siege game mode.
3rd person DISABLED.
Ranked-Casual draft mode 5v5
Picks and Bans
Each team has 2 map bans each game
Higher seed bans map
Lower seed bans map
Higher seed bans map
Lower seed bans map
Game 1: Higher seed chooses a map to play on and first pick or second pick
Game 2: Lower seed chooses a map to play on and first pick or second pick
Game 3: Loser of the previous game chooses a map and first pick or second pick

Team Death Match Rules
Format: Single Elimination best of 1
Note: May change to best of 3 depending on how many teams we have
Finals best of 3
Game setup:
TDM mode.
3rd person DISABLED.
Ranked-Casual draft mode 2V2-5V5
Picks and Bans
Each team has 2 map bans each game
Higher seed bans map
Lower seed bans map
Higher seed bans map
Lower seed bans map
Game 1: Higher seed chooses a map to play on and first pick or second pick
Game 2: Lower seed chooses a map to play on and first pick or second pick
Game 3: Loser of the previous game chooses the map and first pick or second pick
