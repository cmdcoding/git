What is Paladins Realm?

Paladins Realm is a community tournament organizer. We host tournaments for multiple platforms which are free to enter and can win the gem-gift prize pool.

For our player, this is a great place to get started; finding teammates and a competitive playground to test your skills.

For our staff, this is a place to cultivate your skills as a caster, broadcaster or an administrator. We ask that you play competitively if you're trying to win. And if the teams have noobs I will put some good players on their team but fill out the form first thanks.

What do I have to do to compete?

This is easy! Simply go to the tournament page you want to sign up for, fill out the Google Form, join the Discord server and grab a role, then check-in when its time to compete. Keep in mind you have to come with a premade team

Are the tournaments broadcasted?

No, but feel free to record or livestream the tournament just r add a delay in stream.
